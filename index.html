Mental Health Care Analysis Using Data Analytics

Introduction

Mental health issues such as anxiety, depression, and stress are increasingly common among students. Academic pressure, lifestyle imbalance, and lack of sleep significantly affect psychological well-being. This project analyzes student mental health data using data analytics techniques to understand patterns and identify major factors affecting mental health.

Literature Review

  • Academic workload is a primary contributor to stress among students.
  • Sleep deprivation strongly correlates with anxiety and depression.
  • Female students show comparatively higher anxiety levels.
  • Data analytics helps detect mental health trends early.

Problem Statement

To analyze student mental health conditions and identify major factors influencing depression and anxiety using statistical data analysis.

Objectives

  • Analyze distribution of mental health conditions.
  • Identify factors affecting depression and anxiety.
  • Visualize data using charts and graphs.
  • Provide meaningful insights from dataset.

Proposed Methodology

  1. Collect dataset from authentic source.
  2. Load dataset using Python (Pandas).
  3. Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
  4. Perform exploratory data analysis.
  5. Visualize patterns using graphs.
  6. Interpret results and draw conclusions.
