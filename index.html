Student Mental Health Analysis Using Data Analytics

Introduction

Student mental health is an important issue in modern education. Many students experience stress, anxiety, and depression due to academic pressure, social expectations, and lifestyle changes.

This project analyzes student mental health data using basic data analytics techniques such as data cleaning, visualization, and statistical analysis to understand patterns and identify key factors affecting mental health.

Literature Review

  • Studies show that academic workload is a major contributor to student stress.
  • Sleep quality has strong correlation with mental health conditions.
  • Female students often report higher anxiety levels.
  • Data analytics helps identify trends and patterns in mental health data.

Problem Statement

To analyze student mental health data and identify patterns and factors affecting anxiety, depression, and stress levels using data analytics techniques.

Objectives

  • To analyze mental health distribution among students.
  • To identify factors affecting student mental health.
  • To visualize patterns using graphs and charts.
  • To derive meaningful insights from the dataset.

Proposed Methodology

  • Step 1: Collect dataset from authentic source (Kaggle).
  • Step 2: Load dataset using Python and Pandas.
  • Step 3: Perform data cleaning and preprocessing.
  • Step 4: Perform exploratory data analysis.
  • Step 5: Create visualizations using Matplotlib and Seaborn.
  • Step 6: Interpret results and derive insights.
